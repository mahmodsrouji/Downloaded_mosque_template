Required Documents

  • Valid photo ID (Passport/Driving License)
  • Proof of address (Utility bill or bank statement)
  • Civil marriage notice (if applicable)
  • Wali's consent letter (for bride)
Download Checklist

Islamic Requirements

  • Mutual consent of both parties
  • Presence of bride's Wali
  • Two Muslim witnesses
  • Agreed Mahr (dower)

While the Nikah is Islamically valid, couples must also register their marriage with GOV.UK for legal recognition in the UK.
